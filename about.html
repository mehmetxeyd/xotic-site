About

Two crafts,
one way of seeing.

PORTRAIT · PLACEHOLDER

I started behind a camera, learning to find the frame inside a wider scene — what to include, what to cut, where the eye should land first.

Building websites turned out to be the same exercise, just with type and grids instead of light and glass. Every layout is a composition. Every page has a frame.

Now I split my time between the two: shooting personal and client work, and designing & building custom websites for people who'd rather not look like everyone else's template.

A skills section — covering systems and security work, including PKI — is coming to this site soon.
